SECTION 01 // STRATEGIC ALIGNMENT

What We Plan to Achieve and Why

Bridging the gap between R&D's domain expertise and IT's platform capabilities.

This workshop is designed to move from a state of friction and missed opportunities to a focused, collaborative effort that delivers tangible value. By the end of these two days, we will have a clear, actionable roadmap for AI development that will help us close deals, accelerate research, and plan for a successful H2 2026 and H1 2027.

"The core tension to address: R&D knows their pain points but may not know what's technically feasible. IT knows the platform capabilities but may not know which problems matter most. Our job is to create alignment through specificity."

Key Objectives

01 // ALIGN

R&D and IT on 2-3 high-impact AI projects.

02 // DEFINE

Clear, 30-day milestones for each project.

03 // COMMIT

To a shared roadmap for H2 2026 and H1 2027.
