Our Fonts

Our primary fonts are Museo and Source Sans Pro. Museo should be used for all web materials and headlines, whereas Source Sans Pro should be used for all body copy in marketing materials.

In the case of powerpoint presentations or standard office templates where custom installed fonts are not ideal, please use the approved system font, Calibri.

Editorial Themes & Content Guidelines

Converging Forces

The convergence between technologies, ecosystems and processes is deeply interconnected within the enterprise. Real people sit at the epicenter of this convergence, further compounding the complexity in how businesses operate as adaptive enterprises. One small pull in the wrong direction … and failing to take a human-centric approach to transformation … can unravel entire companies.

This means that every decision – from enabling people to embrace new technologies and become evolved experts in their craft to re-engineering operations and shifting ways of working – needs to be carefully considered, grounded in feasibility and skillfully executed. By first understanding how these threads are entwined at the most granular level and then activating diverse networks at the right moments with the right tools, everything will resonate harmoniously. When expertly orchestrated, you’ll spark meaningful, sustained outcomes and create a strong, resilient and adaptive enterprise.

The Modernization Imperative

You need to move at high velocity to grow, optimize and innovate — and move even faster to disrupt amid rapid, unrelenting waves of change. As enterprises trend toward digital and AI-first, agentic business models to reach this velocity in delivering exceptional platforms, products and experiences, leaders must invest in the modernization journey: from upskilling and evolved ways of working to data and cloud infrastructure.

But implementing new capabilities, like AI and ML, throughout the organization doesn’t happen overnight. Organizations must reach a state of perpetual iteration and improvement to scale with intention and drive adoption. To accomplish this, you need modern, dependable, reliable technology as well as pragmatic implementation and talent enablement programs. When you apply a resilient, unified business and technology strategy, you hit momentum to drive quality outcomes continuously and power your future of business … all while delivering measurable, bottom-line results.

Orchestrating Complexity

Fully embracing modernization means welcoming complexity. No modern enterprise is exempt from facing the enormous paradigm shifts that new technologies, customer needs and market trends bring. How you tame, and even master, this complexity will determine how adaptive you’ll be and where you land on the competitive scale. Such an undertaking requires expert orchestration and a systems thinking approach.

Applied systems thinking means activating a diverse network of true experts and deep thinkers to solve the biggest ecosystem-level problems in the world. You need people who know how to work as a system and accept technology as part of that system. This isn’t just about harmonizing the diverse skills of your employees and connecting across enterprise capabilities. It takes a village, and fearless leaders at the helm who strike a healthy balance of optimism and pragmatism, especially in uncertain market conditions.

The Value of Trust

With an abundance of risk and varying degrees of unknowns, our complex realities have ushered us into an era of inherent distrust. But trust is a systemic strength that’s necessary for human centricity. It’s critical for business and your clients’ businesses. Not only do you need to have trust in your people and your teams, but you need trust in your data, your cloud infrastructure, your AI models, your security approach — your entire ecosystem.

Having the ability to expertly orchestrate complexity, embrace modern business practices and platforms, and understand the innerworkings and dependencies of your system enables you to build a business that is worth trusting. When you nurture a trustworthy business, you establish a virtuous cycle of trust with your clients that brings a continuum of loyalty.
